# rebuild-landing-visual-sections

## Why

The landing now says the right things, but its visuals are still structurally close to the generic template. Zenlift needs to feel like a premium dark workout tracker with product UI examples, not a SaaS dashboard or pricing page.

## What Changes

- Add a stronger hero composition with a workout app mockup.
- Rebuild cards around app-like workout state, previous weights, progress insights, and routine structures.
- Improve responsive spacing, section hierarchy, and visual consistency.
- Keep the homepage product-first and keep privacy out of the feature narrative.

## Non-Goals

- No privacy route work.
- No real Play Store URL.
- No complete mobile app screenshots.
