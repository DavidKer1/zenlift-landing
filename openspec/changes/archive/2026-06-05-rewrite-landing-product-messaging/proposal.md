# rewrite-landing-product-messaging

## Why

The current page copy sells a generic SaaS platform with pricing, trials, demos, fake testimonials, and fake company logos. Zenlift needs product copy based on the blueprint: routines, set logging, previous lifts, workout history, progress, and real gym usage.

## What Changes

- Rewrite hero, problem, features, progress examples, routines, and final CTA copy.
- Remove unsupported claims and fabricated proof.
- Keep the page in English.
- Use `Download on Google Play` with placeholder `#` href.
- Keep privacy out of the homepage feature narrative.

## Non-Goals

- No final visual polish beyond messaging and section intent.
- No complete privacy policy.
- No privacy feature section.
