# Design

The product narrative follows this order: hero, problem, fast tracking, progress, routines, final CTA. The page should sound honest and product-ready while avoiding unsupported claims such as fastest app, AI coach, guaranteed strength gains, or proven user outcomes.

Existing section component names can remain for now to keep implementation scoped, but the content and IDs should shift toward the approved page structure: `#features`, `#progress`, and `#routines`.
